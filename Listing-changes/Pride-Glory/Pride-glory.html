WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP BOXING
Larry Holmes vs. Jerry Cooney
THE PRIDE AND THE GLORY

A last minute addition to HBO.

On June 11, 1982, Larry Holmes fought Jerry Cooney in the fight dubbed, "The Pride and the Glory." According to Wikipedia, the fight was viewed live on closed-circuit television and pay-per-view. HBO then acquired the rights to re-broadcast it, which it did on the 18th and 20th.

These re-broadcasts replaced scheduled airings of "High Risk" on June 18th and "Silver Streak" June 20th.
