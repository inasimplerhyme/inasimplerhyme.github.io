My name is Jon, and I am currently a human. That being the case, I make mistakes! If you happen to find one, I'd love to hear from you and correct it!

One thing you might notice is that if you search up a program on my site and compare the dates aired to a vintage HBO guide, it might not always match. That's actually not a mistake. HBO considered the the start of their broadcasting day to be around 6 or 7 am. So if something aired at 4 am on the morning of the 8th of December, in the guide, it would be listed at Dec. 7th. For the purposes of making a spreadsheet which tracked when everything aired, it was just so much easier to have the start of the day be midnight, as it actually is, rather than have it be 6 or 7 am, depending on the day. So if something aired twice a day, but that second airing was after midnight, on my site, it's listed as airing the next day.

But any mistakes you might notice are welcome, or, if you just want to share your HBO experiences, feel free to pass them along. I'd love to hear from you.
