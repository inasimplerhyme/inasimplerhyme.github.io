My name is Jon, and I am currently a human. That being the case, I make mistakes! If you happen to find one, I'd love to hear from you and correct it!

When you look on my site and see a movie's air dates displayed as something like, Aug 3,5,9,12, etc, I didn't simply copy that from the guide. I created a spreadsheet with a schedule of every month in every year, and then used cell formulas to calculate every date a movie aired from that schedule. Using this method, there were even a few instances where I found mistakes in the guides! So if they made some, I won't beat myself up over the fact that my site will have some too. So any mistakes you might notice are welcome, just hit me up and I'll correct them as soon as possible. Or, if you just want to share your HBO watching experiences, feel free to pass them along. I'd love to hear from you!
