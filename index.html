Jon Schell

Re-recording Mixer, Sound Designer, Audio Editor

Decades of Experience

I’ve been fortunate enough to have been nominated for an Emmy multiple times in my 25-plus years of working in audio-post. Some of my most recent shows have been Welcome to Wrexham and RuPaul’s Drag Race. I’ve also worked on hit-reality shows like Below Deck, Million Dollar Listing, popular animated shows like Rick and Morty, Solar Opposites, and Duncanville, and also the films Beavis and Butt-Head Do the Universe, Blue’s Big City Adventure, and Reno 911!: The Hunt for QAnon.
