The mystery of July 15, 1982

What aired that evening?

The HBO Guide for July of 1982 has the Hagler vs. Hearns fight listed at 9 pm, but it never happened. Thomas Hearns initially dropped out and was replaced by Fulgencio Obelmejias, but then that was cancelled due to an injury from Hagler and rescheduled for Oct. 30th. This happened after the guides were set to print, so trying to figure out what HBO actually aired that night is a bit of a mystery due to conflicting sources.
